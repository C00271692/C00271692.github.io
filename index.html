About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Kacper Krakowiak, a Software Developer and Cyber Data Engineer currently studying a Masters of Computing – (Artificial Intelligence) at Dublin City University (DCU).

In my Masters I am building a strong foundation in AI with a heavy focus on data: cleaning, preprocessing, transforming and validating datasets so they are ready for machine learning. I care a lot about data quality, because I know that poor data almost always leads to poor AI results.

Before starting my Masters I completed a Software Development degree where I worked on projects ranging from predictive algorithms and neural networks to data visualisation. My final-year project used Genetic Algorithms to optimise test case generation for C code in Sikraken, and during my year-and-a-half internship as a Cyber Data Engineer at State Street I gained hands-on experience with Splunk, AWS and Python on real-world security and data challenges.

I'm excited to move into roles where I can combine solid data engineering practices with practical machine learning and AI – whether that’s building reliable data pipelines, experimenting with models, or turning ideas into production ready solutions.
